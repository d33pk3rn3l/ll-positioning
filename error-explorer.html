BACK-OF-ENVELOPE PRINCIPLES

Anchored dead reckoning (Brownian bridge)

Between two control fixes at distances d₁ behind and d₂ ahead:

d_eff = d₁·d₂ / (d₁+d₂)    (d₂→∞ ⇒ d_eff = d₁)

σ = √( (k·d_eff)² + σ²_ctrl )

k per axis: along-track = odometer scale-factor error [%·d]; cross-track = azimuth error ψ [rad]·d; up = height-transfer error [%·d].

Datum tie (local → ME frame)

σ_ME = √( σ²_local + σ²_datum )

σ_datum ≈ 10 m horiz / 2 m vert (lander located in LRO NAC).

Free-air sensitivity (the requirement driver)

∂g/∂h = 2g/R = 187 µGal/m

50 µGal ⇒ σ_U ≤ 0.27 m; budget allocation ⇒ σ_U ≈ 0.10 m (19 µGal).

Lava tube signal (∞ horizontal cylinder)

Δg = 2πG·Δρ·r² / z

r=30 m, z=60 m, Δρ=−3000 kg/m³ ⇒ ≈ −1.9 mGal peak (S/N ≳ 40).

Max horizontal gradient (3√3/8)·Δg/z ≈ 16 µGal/m ⇒ σ_horiz ~1–2 m suffices.

Other budget terms

tilt: δg = g(1−cos θ) → 10 µGal @ 72″

Earth tide: ±400 µGal/month → modelled, ~5 µGal residual

budget: RSS of terms ≈ 41 µGal < 50 µGal ✓
